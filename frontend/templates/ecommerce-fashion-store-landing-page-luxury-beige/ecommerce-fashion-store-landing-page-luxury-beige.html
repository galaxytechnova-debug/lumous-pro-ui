Our Story

"Elegance is when the inside is as beautiful as the outside."

— Coco Chanel

Founded in Paris in 2015, Maison Eclat emerged from a singular vision: to create clothing that speaks in whispers rather than shouts. Our atelier on Rue Saint-Honoré continues a tradition of uncompromising craftsmanship.

Each garment requires over 40 hours of meticulous handwork. We source fabrics from heritage mills in Italy and Japan, working exclusively with natural fibers that age beautifully.

Our commitment to slow fashion means producing only what is needed, when it is needed.
